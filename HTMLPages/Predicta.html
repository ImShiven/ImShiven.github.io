PREDICTA

background

PREDICTA is an application which is built solely for Team-Engagement activities and based on game of Cricket. Participants are required to make some predictions before starting of the match and each prediction has some points weightage. At the end of the match, participants are awarded with fixed points for their correct pedictions.

Say, there is a match between teams- INDIA(IND) vs SOUTH AFRICA (RSA) and anchor declares the predictions (to be made) as follows :
  • Which team will win the TOSS?
  • Which team will win the MATCH?
  • Who will be Man Of The Match?

Participants are supposed to make predictions for these scenarios and awarded with points for each correct predictions.
